Understanding UV & SPF

What is UV Index?

  • 0-2 (Low): Safe. No protection needed.
  • 3-7 (Mod/High): Protection required.
  • 8+ (Very High): Extra protection essential.

Why SPF Matters?

SPF (Sun Protection Factor) measures how well sunscreen protects you from UVB rays.

  • SPF 15: Blocks ~93% of UVB rays.
  • SPF 30: Blocks ~97% of UVB rays.
  • SPF 50: Blocks ~98% of UVB rays.
